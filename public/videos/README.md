# Hero Background Video

Place your hero section background video here.

**File name:** `hero-background.mp4`

**Recommended specifications:**

- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- Duration: 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- File size: Under 5MB for optimal performance
- Content: Abstract technology, team collaboration, or professional workspace

**How to add your video:**

1. Place your video file in this directory (`public/videos/`)
2. Name it exactly: `hero-background.mp4`
3. The video will automatically play as background in the hero section

**Alternative options:**

- Use a video from a CDN or online source by updating the video source URL in `src/components/sections/hero.tsx`
- Use free stock videos from sites like Pexels, Pixabay, or Unsplash

**Note:** The video will be automatically muted and looped, with dark overlays for text readability.
